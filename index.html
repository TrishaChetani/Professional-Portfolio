Tips to improve quality for project

  • A definition of quality for your team and project
  • Define the proper metrics by having a agreement to meet the goals, for examples: Get the feedback from stakeholder and coworkers and share this as a romm for improvement
  • Talk to stakeholder and find out what working out and never change the same
  • Achieve objectives by having proper planning and breakdown and deliverables. For example Sprint Planning, Retrospectives at team level as well as project level
  • Have ownership of task by having a good communication with relevant stakeholders :Daily Scrum, Share test reports, Involve developer/Tester to review your automated test cases/Test plan etc and incorporate comment.
  • Set up the process to evaluate quality before launching the new version for release cycle: Share your viewpoints and have a agreement on a process, place, time for multiple perspectives to be included
  • Keep on identifying the opportunities for improvement
  • Working on testing innovations by generate new ideas and insights for the team and project.
  • Reporting the results to the relevant stakeholders.
  • Combine the knowledge of many individuals. For examples, having the shared knowledge among coworkers.
  • Create an environment for feedback where the input from people at all levels is valued. For example Dogfooding, Internal Bug Bash with team, Test Plan review, Automated test review, Usability Test for the project.
  • Create a quality checks for each projects and share to the coworkers :Unit testing, static analysis, Identify testability improvement for the system.
  • Have a operational excellence (For a day/Bi-weekly/Monthly). This is place where all the team member work on operational issue of project by share the improvement the achieved.
  • Production Issue: having a list of production issue by proactively mitigation using the root cause analysis techniques.(Identify the cause and find the approach and make sure issue will not happen again).
  • Have a loop where different stakeholder(Business, PM, QA, DEV, DESIGNER, SALES etc) can give continuous feedback, improvement about project/domain/Individuals/Team
  • Fast Automation of new features and existing features.
  • Prioritize the issue which are not fixed and pending from the long time during the project phase.(May be monthly once)
  • Involve earlier in project requirement discussion and test early.
  • Worked on non-functional issues in your project. i.e, Security, Performance, Memory Leaks, ANR- Application is not responsive, monitoring system. Share to the team the identified non functional issues by (Perform monthly once -depends what is suitable to the team) and a whole business critical scenario/new launched products. (Though we have tested and done sign off when project is signed off).
  • Asked new joiner for project feedback/improvement they could think off.
  • Define Minimal acceptance test for your project and make sure it is executed(Manual/Automated) by dev before starting the test. For example, During handing over of new develop feature to software testers.
  • Add online monitoring and alerts, shared outcomes to relevant people by doing root cause analysis
